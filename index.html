Some guiding principles to consider as you migrate to distance learning include:

  • Review your assignments as they relate to learning outcomes and consider where it is possible to use remote modalities to deliver the content and uphold course integrity.
  • Take advantage of the online resources compiled by library staff.
  • Communicate with your chair for additional support on how to migrate to online teaching.
  • Consider necessary changes to attendance policies based on the current situation and revise your syllabus accordingly. If necessary, please recalibrate your attendance policies for the remainder of the semester, being flexible where possible for COVID-19 concerns and the likelihood of reduced access during online instruction. Unexcused absences should remain unexcused; however, I request that you be understanding about what constitutes an unexcused absence.
  • Make contingency plans for any off-campus activity/field trip you may have planned for the duration of the semester.
